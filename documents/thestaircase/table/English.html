7.0The Goal is not to win now. The Goal is to improve now. Do not try to win now.
Try to improve now, and you will win later.

.

TheStaircase is a training system designed to help you reach your league goal in the most efficient way possible. Choose the league you want to reach below and follow the corresponding benchmarks to achieve this. Keep in mind, that this should be your long term goal and is often times not just one league above your current league.

.

.

.

TheStaircase (How to)

.

1Choose a Race.

.

2Choose a League Goal.

.

3Start at Step 0 if Zerg. Start at Step 1 if Terran or Protoss.

.

4Follow the constrictions for each step.

.

5To pass a game, you must meet or exceed all benchmarks in your league goal.

.

6When you pass 4 out of 5 games you move onto the next step.

.

Guidelines/Notes

.

.

 - Play aggressively; avoid static defenses

.

 Benchmark Key - Experiment with the new unit/units on your current Step as much as possible!

.

 - Focus as much as possible on your goals, and not on winning the game.

.

Spending SkillAn equation that measures the skill of keeping your money low relative to your income. - Avoid arrow keys or moving the mouse to the edge of the screen to move.

.

Saturation SpeedThe time it takes from base completion to 16 workers on minerals - Click on the minimap and use location keys to move screen

.

Benchmark CalculationGGTracker.com - Use Hotkeys, Control Groups, and Camera Location keys as much as possible.

.

Consistency RequirementMust hit both Benchmarks 4 out of 5 games to PASSTry to get 48 workers on Step 1 (3 base saturation)

.

Try to get 66 workers on all other Steps (3 base saturation) NOTE: only take the gas you need when you need it!!!

.
